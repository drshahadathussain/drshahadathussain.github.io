Professional Summary

Advanced Materials Scientist & Additive Manufacturing Engineer with 06+ years of R&D experience across top-tier UAE institutions (NYU Abu Dhabi, Khalifa University) and CSIR-AMPRI India. Expert in laser powder bed fusion of NiTi shape memory alloys, TPMS lattice metamaterials, and carbon-fiber composite additive manufacturing. Strong track record: 15+ journal articles, 450+ citations, h-index 10, peer reviewer for 15+ leading journals (Additive Manufacturing, Materials & Design). Combines materials characterization (SEM, XRD, DSC, EBSD) with data science (Python, ML) to drive process-structure-property correlations for Industry 4.0. Seeking industrial R&D leadership roles to translate advanced materials into scalable manufacturing solutions.
