About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Khalifa University (QS World Ranking 181) working at the Advanced Digital & Additive Manufacturing (ADAM) Center. My current research focuses on laser powder bed fusion (LPBF) of NiTi shape memory alloys, triply periodic minimal surface (TPMS) lattices (Schwartz Primitive & Gyroid), fatigue behavior, and microstructural characterization.

I have published several high-impact papers, collaborated with University of Thessaly (Greece) and CSIR-AMPRI (India), and have 228+ citations (h-index 8).
