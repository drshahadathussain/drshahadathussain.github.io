Professional Summary

Postdoctoral fellow at Khalifa University (QS Rank: 181) actively engaged in additive manufacturing of NiTi triply periodic minimal surface (TPMS) structures. Collaborating with Advanced Digital and Additive Manufacturing Center (ADAM) and international institutions including University of Thessaly (Greece) and CSIR-AMPRI (India).
